Triangle Tracker

Triangle tracker is an ideal program designed to show the type of triangle based on the dimension given. To give you an answer to the type of triangle according to what the three values can make.

Types of triangles include:

  • Scalene triangle
  • Equilateral triangle
  • Isoscles triangle

About Program

This program helps a person know the type of a triangle based on its dimensions.

An equilateral triangle has all the sides equal.

An isosceles triangles has only two sides equal.

A scalene triangle has none of the sides equal. However the sum of any two sides of the triange must be greater than the third side.
